What is the PEKKA?

  • A powerful single-target melee unit.

  • Wears heavy armor and carries a huge sword.

  • Known for very high damage per hit and high hitpoints.

  • Slow movement and attack speed, but devastating when it connects.

What does “P.E.K.K.A” stand for?

Supercell hasn’t given an official canonical meaning, but fans often joke about it. In one official poll, the most popular community answer was “Perfectly Enraged Knight Killer of Assassins.”
The creators often play along with the mystery.

In Clash of Clans

  • Unlocked in the Barracks.

  • Great for destroying defenses due to high damage.

  • Expensive in elixir.
